# Equipment Set-up

## Storage

The removable equipment is stored in the bottom left locker at the 3T (code 1507).

**Electrical stimulation:**

* [ ] DS7, ground cable, TTL cable, power cable
* [ ] Disposable electrodes, gel
* [ ] The leads for stimulation are kept plugged in at the scanner, on either side of the filter plate

**Physiological recording:**

* [ ] Biopac modules, ethernet cable, ribbon cable, power cable

**Presentation hardware:**

* [ ] 2 laptops, 1 docking station, 2 power cables, 1 Acqknowledge dongle
* [ ] Button boxes are hanging on the side of the scanner

## Start of each testing day

1. Install equipment as shown above
2. Turn on projector using the scanner Stim PC (Desktop > Projector Control > ‘On’)
3. In equipment rack, switch projector video switch to ‘Laptop’ and switch response box switch to PC2
4. Change keyboard of Stim PC to ENG (to receive scanner triggers and button presses)
